About Us

Emmy-liz is a non-profit organization dedicated to addressing the unique needs of individuals dealing with various health issues and chronic diseases. To achieve this mission, the Emmy-Liz Foundation will award health insurance in support of the following activities. Also, the NGO will work in partnership with communities, government, donors, and the private sector, for universal health coverage and social justice for every Nigerian. Direct medical services will be provided by a medical outreach program organized. Creating a Mentally Aware Nigeria. Health and nutrition education Antual prescription refill

Our Mission

Emmy-Liz mission is to measurably improve the health and well-being of individuals, families, and communities in our service area by empowering individuals, and families to create a healthy lifestyle to prevent chronic health problems. Touching lives one person at a time.

Our Vision

Emmy-liz is dedicated to addressing the unique needs of individuals/families facing various health issues Supporting the underserved community by helping people pay more attention to their health. Providing medical outreach programs for preventative care Understanding and addressing immediate needs while investing in long-term systems change with a health equity frame. Increasing the knowledge about medical resources, and health Education to improve the health and well-being of individuals and families in the community.

Meet The Team

Ijeoma Ibe is the president & CEO of Emmy-liz Divine Medical Mission. Her passion for health care has driven her to open this NGO to give back to her community, and touch lives one person at a time. She is a Registered Nurse with a Master's in psychiatry mental health Nurse practitioner (PMHNP). Ijeoma Ibe has been a nurse for over ten years. Her nursing career started in Nigeria where she practiced as a registered nurse with an associate degree. She later traveled to the United States to advance her career in nursing. She currently holds a master's in nursing as a psychiatric nurse practitioner.
